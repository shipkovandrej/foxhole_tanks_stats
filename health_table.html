    Versioning

    This article is considered accurate for the current version (1.57) of the game.


    Description

    This spreadsheet shows how many of X you need to disable or destroy Y.

    For firearms, the number shown is the minimum amount of bullets needed when at closest range without missing.

    For weapons with RNG damage the values show min damage - max damage.

    The values are for penetrating hits only, on standard version of the vehicle, using the standard version of the ammo type and not high velocity variants (or weaker variants).

    For artillery and mortar, the values are for direct hits.

    The values are rounded up to the nearest integer, 7.1 becomes 8.

    The value in grey is to disable the vehicle while the value in white is to kill.

    The columns are color-coded depending on the damage types.

    Some weapons are skipped for certain vehicles for various reasons:

    • For armored vehicles, the small arms weapon are not shown because they are very inefficient (only 1 damage per shot), the shrapnel weapons are also inefficient
    • For armored vehicles, the flame-based weapons are not shown because they do not inflict any damage to them.
    • The deployed explosives (except the AT Mine) are not shown because they are too difficult to use in any real fight and also inefficient.
